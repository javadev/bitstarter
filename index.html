Ticket assistant is a web based mobile application which helps You managing all kinds of electronic tickets, passes, discounts and etc. It stores all your tickets in one place and helps to schedule all the events You go for.
You can use and validate your tickets or passes directly from the application. It helps to save money and time, and save the environment by making unnecessary to print out your ticket.
By scheduling all the events it helps You to don't miss any important date and being well prepered for anything.
